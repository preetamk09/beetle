Join Our Team

At Beetle, we believe that our success is driven by the talent, dedication, and passion of our team members. We are always on the lookout for exceptional individuals who share our enthusiasm for drone technology and want to contribute to our mission of revolutionizing industries. Join us and be part of an exciting journey at the forefront of innovation.

Why Work with Us?

Innovation and Impact: At Beetle, you will have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge drone technology and contribute to its development. Your work will have a direct impact on various industries, pushing boundaries and shaping the future of unmanned aerial vehicles.

Collaborative Environment: We foster a collaborative and inclusive work culture where every team member's ideas and contributions are valued. You'll work alongside talented professionals from diverse backgrounds, inspiring each other to achieve greatness.

Continuous Learning: We encourage a growth mindset and provide ample opportunities for learning and development. You'll have access to training programs, workshops, and industry events that will enhance your skills and keep you at the forefront of the drone industry.

Exciting Projects: Join us in tackling exciting projects and solving complex challenges. From designing next-generation drones to developing advanced software and systems, you'll be part of groundbreaking initiatives that push the boundaries of what drones can do.

Open Positions

We are currently seeking talented individuals to join our team in the following roles:

1. Drone Engineer: As a drone engineer, you will be responsible for designing and developing innovative drone systems, including hardware and software components. You should have a strong background in engineering principles and a passion for pushing the limits of drone technology.

2. Software Developer: In this role, you will contribute to the development of drone software applications, including flight control systems, data processing algorithms, and user interfaces. Proficiency in programming languages and experience with drone software frameworks is required.

3. Sales and Marketing Specialist: As part of our sales and marketing team, you will play a crucial role in promoting our drone solutions, identifying new business opportunities, and building relationships with clients. Strong communication and presentation skills are essential.

How to Apply

To apply for a position at Beetle, please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant portfolio or work samples to careers@beetle.com. In your cover letter, please include why you are interested in joining our team and how your skills and experience align with the role.
